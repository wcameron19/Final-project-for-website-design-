    My name is Wyatt Cameron. I'm a 22 year old College student currently enrolled at New England College. I'm from a small town in Vermont named Salisbury.

    I'm an athlete at NEC, in the summer time I spend everyday playing for the Vermont Lake Monsters in Burlington Vermont. Being from Vermont it is honestly a dream come true to play for them.

    Growing up I always did outdoorsy stuff with my father like hunting and fishing. This is really wear I got my love for the outdoors and all that it offers us.

    Deer hunting can by slow and a lot of work but it is very rewarding when you finally get one. You can shoot deer with rifles, shotguns and muzzleloaders.

    Duck hunting is more action but it can be hard to hit your shots even if your familiar with firearms. Duck hunting requires you to use a shotgun with steel shot only

    Ice fishing has been a hobby of mine since I was a little kid, it's nothing like land fishing cause you can go where ever to fish.
